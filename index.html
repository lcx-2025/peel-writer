📖 Writing Tool Usage Rules

✨ Basic Operations

  • Select Template: Choose PEEL/PEEEL/PETAL/PEELL for your writing structure
  • Edit Sections: Fill in each section (Point/Evidence/Explain/Link) with your content
  • Word Count: Real-time word count for each section and total

🔧 Section Controls

  • Add Section: Click to insert a new section after the selected block
  • Delete Section: Select a section first, then click to delete (core sections cannot be deleted)
  • Rename Section: Click the ✏️ icon next to the section name to rename
  • Drag to Reorder: Hold the ≡ icon on the left to drag and reorder sections

📤 Export & Copy

  • Copy Full Text: One-click copy your complete writing
  • Export TXT: Download your writing as a .txt file (filename = essay title)

📜 History & Feedback

  • Submit & Get Feedback: Get instant scoring and improvement suggestions
  • Writing History: View, load, or delete your past writing records
  • Load to Editor: Restore a past record to continue editing (full structure preserved)

🎨 Theme

  • Dark/Light Mode: Click the top-right button to switch themes (auto-saved)
